## Why

Kevin's legacy fixture (answered Q-T1ŌĆōQ-T8, never the Q-TB binaries) has a
follow-up missing set of `{4 binary_pick, 2 binary_pick_derived, 1
multiselect_derived, 1 freeform}`. CC-170 renders the derived types when their
parents resolve ŌĆö but Kevin's binary_pick_derived parents ARE the 4 Q-TB
`binary_pick` questions, which he never answered. And those 4 `binary_pick`
questions are **self-contained** (`items: RankingItem[]` length 2 ŌĆö a fixed
attitude pair, no `derived_from`, no parent resolution), yet the follow-up
page's `renderableMissing` filter never included non-derived `binary_pick`.

So the most important typing questions ŌĆö the attitude binaries that ARE the
Lens read ŌĆö sit deferred. Rendering them lets a legacy user upgrade to the
binary typing format via the follow-up link (and, on a subsequent pass once
they're answered, unblocks the binary_pick_derived dominance questions too).

## The change ŌĆö `app/follow-up/[token]/page.tsx`

1. Add `"binary_pick"` to the `renderableMissing` filter (alongside the types
   CC-170 added). It needs NO parent resolution ŌĆö its `items` are already on
   the question definition.
2. In `GapFillBlock`, render a `binary_pick` question via `SinglePickPicker`
   (already imported; it's the same 2-item single-select component used for
   `binary_pick_derived`). Pass `question.items` directly.
3. In `buildGapFillAnswers`, emit a `SinglePickAnswer` for the binary_pick
   (pick one of two; rank-1 signal) ŌĆö the SAME shape CC-170 already emits for
   `binary_pick_derived`, and the shape `saveSession.ts` + the engine already
   accept. Merge-by-`question_id`, no double-append.
4. The deferred-count note now only covers genuinely-unresolvable items
   (derived questions whose parents are still unanswered). Keep the plain-
   language copy from CC-170.

- **binary_pick_derived needs a second pass.** Derived items resolve from

  A future enhancement could re-resolve derived questions live as their parents
  are answered in-session (mirroring the assessment in-flow behavior) to make

  `ranking_derived` themselves. Chaining derived-from-derived is a separate,
  larger change. Out of scope.
