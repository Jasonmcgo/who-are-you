# CC-164.1-TARGET-ZONE-NE-QUADRANT

> Cowork-chat CC, 2026-05-24. Correction to CC-164, pre-push (CC-164 not
> yet pushed). Local visual review showed the Target Zone drawn as the
> full 42–58° angular wedge from origin — it bleeds through the center
> and overlaps the SW Drift / Grip Zone. Wrong shape.

## What's wrong

CC-164 shaded the **angular band** (a wedge emanating from origin out to
the plot edge). That fills the middle of the chart and reaches back
toward the origin, so the "target" reads as a corridor the whole line
already sits inside — not as a destination.

## What it should be

The Target Zone is the **destination**: the high-Goal + high-Soul region
— the **NE quadrant**, labeled "Giving / Presence" (a.k.a. Purposeful /
Presence). It's the *potential reach* you're aiming for; as Grip drops,
the usable line extends out toward it. It must NOT reach toward origin
and must NOT overlap the SW Grip Zone.

## Geometry — replace the wedge with the NE quadrant box

In `lib/trajectoryChart.ts`, change `svgTargetZoneWedge()` (rename to
`svgTargetZoneQuadrant()`) so the polygon is the upper-right quadrant in
SCORE space — Goal ≥ 50 AND Soul ≥ 50:

Vertices (score space), mapped through `mapToSvg`:
- `(50, 50)`  → mapToSvg → `(160, 124)`
- `(100, 50)` → `(260, 124)`
- `(100, 100)`→ `(260, 24)`
- `(50, 100)` → `(160, 24)`

That's the upper-right quarter of the plot box. Keep the green fill +
opacity. Update `data-element` to `"target-zone-quadrant"`.

The `50` quadrant threshold is the same score-space value as the Grip
Zone radius (`GRIP_ZONE_SCORE_RADIUS = 50`) — they're mirror regions
(SW low-magnitude triangle vs NE high-magnitude quadrant). Define a
constant `TARGET_ZONE_SCORE_FLOOR = 50` and build the box from it (don't
hardcode 50 inline) so the two zones stay tied to one number.

Remove the now-unused band-degree constants
(`TARGET_ZONE_LOWER_DEG` / `TARGET_ZONE_UPPER_DEG`) and the ray-exit
helper added in CC-164 — the quadrant box doesn't need them.
