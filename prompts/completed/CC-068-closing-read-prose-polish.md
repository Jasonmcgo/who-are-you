## One Note on Method

The bridge requirement is the spine of this CC. Every closing must point at a next movement, not just describe a quadrant. If you find yourself writing a beautiful closing that lacks a bridge sentence, rewrite — even at the cost of some elegance. A description without a bridge reads as a verdict, and a verdict is colder than the product wants.

Synthesis-over-contradiction (memory: `feedback_synthesis_over_contradiction.md`) applies pervasively. The Give closing should sound coherent and warm — your verbs and your nouns are pulling the same direction, and the closing gets to say so. The Striving, Longing, and Parallel Lives closings should name the gap kindly and point at the bridge across it. The Gripping closing should soften toward "a season rather than a shape" rather than a stable identity claim. The Neutral closing should explicitly decline to render a verdict. None of the six templates should make the user feel diagnosed.

The Striving template carries the most architectural weight. It must work standalone for clean Goal-high / Soul-thin cases AND it must accept a future Defensive Builder kicker for Goal-high + Gripping-cluster cases. Write it as if it might be wrapped — leave space.
