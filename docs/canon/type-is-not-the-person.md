# Canon: The type is not the person — trajectory is the identity

**Status:** Canon (2026-07-03, owner-affirmed). Governs every render, prose, and typing decision
downstream. When any other spec conflicts with this, this wins on product intent.

## The principle

A cognitive-function type (MBTI code, dominant/auxiliary stack) is a **shared coordinate, not an
identity.** It locates a person on one axis. It does not tell you who they are.

The identity — the thing "Who Are You?" exists to surface — is the **trajectory**: Goal / Soul / Aim
/ Grip, the gifts, the drifts, the season, the likely false path, and the destination at the 50°
intersection of Goal and Soul. That is the differentiator, and it is the moat. No 16-type instrument
produces it.

## The rule (load-bearing for rendering)

**Two people of the same type must never render the same.**

The type is a small **guidance layer worn over the trajectory** — never the headline. Same spine,
radically different edges. If the report, the shape read, or a game card ever makes two same-type
people feel interchangeable, it has failed at the one thing the product exists to do.

Flattening two same-type people into one description is the *same failure* as the INTJ attractor
(defaulting everyone to one type) — just wearing different clothes. Both erase the person.

## The evidence

**Nat and JDrew are both Fi-Se / ISFP — and they are nothing alike.**
- **Nat** — the quiet maker. Her grip is *Security*: "will the foundation still hold what I'm
  carrying?" She fixes the broken thing before you notice it was broken.
- **JDrew** — the quiet moral essayist. Justice-driven, delayed precision, the one calm true sentence
  after everyone else has stopped talking.

Same type. Completely different souls, loads, and destinations. The 16-type label is the *only* thing
about them that is identical. Everything that actually *is* them lives in the trajectory.

## Corollary — typing is hygiene, not the product

Getting the type right matters only so it stops actively lying (see the Fi-Se attractor fix in
CC-188). That is **hygiene / table stakes.** It is not the product and not the moat. Effort and
narrative weight belong on the trajectory layer, not on perfecting the label.

## Genesis

The product exists because people are misread *by their type* — someone reads the label and believes
they have the person. The promise is the opposite: *no — here is your actual shape, and your actual
road.* Two people sharing a type and being unmistakably different is that promise, kept.

## Render implications

- Lead with the trajectory / unique shape. Demote the MBTI code to guidance-language (secondary /
  litmus), per the two-tier render canon.
- Any two same-type sessions must **diverge** on Goal/Soul/Aim/Grip, gifts, season, and destination —
  never converge on type-templated prose.
- "Clear spine, rich edges" applies at the person level, not just within an axis: shared spine (the
  type), unique edges (the whole trajectory).
