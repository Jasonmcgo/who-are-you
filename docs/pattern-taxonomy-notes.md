# Pattern Taxonomy — design notes (not yet built)

**Status:** Captured intent, parked. Do NOT rewrite the landing-page "10 life
patterns" yet (owner decision 2026-05-23). The current 10 are a transitional
recognition device; the real system is engine-derived. Stickiness/accuracy is
empirical and 14 cohorts is too thin to validate ~10 patterns (~1 each).

## What a pattern actually is (owner framing)

Patterns are **gsTAG geometry + the next honest step** — not named characters,
not MBTI-disguised types. Defined by the presence/absence of the forces and by
movement magnitude × Grip, each pattern surfaces the *need* it implies.

Count is open — maybe not 10 ("not sure if that's too many or not enough").

### The regions that matter (owner's list)
- **Soul + Goal, low Aim** — movement present but unaimed.
- **Soul without Goal** — love/meaning without built form.
- **Goal without Soul** — building without the beloved object.
- **High movement + high Grip** — moving hard but fear is steering.
- **Low movement + low Grip** — quiet on both; little drag, little motion.

### The needs each region should name (the "next honest step")
- **Escape FUD** — high-Grip patterns: the work is releasing fear's grip on the wheel.
- **Gain direction** — raise Aim: movement exists but isn't governed/aimed.
- **Increase trajectory** — Goal-oriented people who need more/longer movement.
- **Release Grip** — balanced trajectory that could move *more* if Grip eased.

## Endgame (the real solution)
An **engine-derived overarching label** computed from gsTAG (Goal / Soul /
Trajectory / Aim / Grip) + the **9 body cards** (Lens, Compass, Hands, Voice,
Gravity, Trust, Weather, Fire, Path) — themselves derived from MBTI-style
function pairs, OCEAN, and Grip/primal-question patterns. Likely hundreds of
unique human patterns. Requires: (a) a typing-accuracy pass first (several
cohorts are mistyped today), (b) more humans to validate resonance.

## Revisit plan (owner, 2026-05-23)

- **Trigger:** revisit the 10 (or N) pattern types as we approach **~50 cohorts**
  — enough humans to validate resonance, which 14 can't.
- **Design principle — big recognizable boxes.** The patterns should be broad,
  clean "large boxes" a *typical* human can immediately self-identify with
  ("that's me / that's someone I love"). Optimize for recognizability over
  precision; the box is a recognition device, not a fine-grained type.
- **Prescriptions stay individual.** The "next honest step" / growth path is
  **tailored per individual** (engine-derived from their gsTAG + 9 cards), NOT
  per box. So: a shared, recognizable pattern box + a per-person prescription.

## Why we are NOT shipping a single archetype/Lens label now
3–4 archetype buckets can't carry an accurate "who you are" label (an INFJ
routes to the same bucket as Jason). The single-word Lens labels
(artist/pastor/catalyst…) over-claim a persona that collides with real lives
(e.g. "the pastor" for an atheist). Until the engine can earn a per-person
label, the Individual leads with the per-person **epigraph**, not a label.
